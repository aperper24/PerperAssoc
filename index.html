Our Work

Proven in the Field

Emergency Management

COVID-19 Pandemic Response & Recovery

Partnered with Epicenter-Innovation beginning March 2020 to provide ICS training, mentoring and operational planning for Alternate Care Site (ACS) operations across multiple jurisdictions during the global pandemic response.

Supported planning and logistics section operations, lessons learned documentation and reopening coordination frameworks for healthcare and community organizations.

Outcome: Operationalized ACS facilities and delivered ICS/NIMS-compliant response structures under real-world crisis conditions.

Business Continuity

Disaster Recovery Plan for AMP Communications

Developed a comprehensive Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP) for AMP Communications, consolidating all critical business continuity information into a single, actionable repository.

Defined clear recovery processes, escalation paths and communication protocols for their leadership and operations teams.

Outcome: Fully documented DRP enabling leadership to manage any disaster scenario with clarity and confidence.

Special Event Management

Harry Potter: Wizards Unite Fan Festival

Developed Medical Operations and Event Incident Management Plans for a 10,000+ attendee special event at White River State Park, Indianapolis. Provided a flexible EMS command framework for Indianapolis EMS Division and Event Medics.

Plans addressed mass casualty response, inter-agency coordination, staging logistics and real-time incident communication across the multi-day event.

Outcome: Safe, well-coordinated event operations with a clear incident management framework across all responding agencies.
